Welcome to Timeless Advisor.

The game that will test your knowledge of History by throwing you back in time to become an advisor of some of History's most famous characters.

Once you have chosen your preferred character, you will jump back in time to some of their most pivotal moments and will need to advise them on the best course of action for each event.

Be careful though, like all good advisors, sometimes you won't be listened to - and if you give the wrong advice three times you will be sent to the dungeon without mercy.
